Overview

The purpose of this website is to provide Computer Science Resources and Opportunities to students that are currently in Middle School or High School providing pages with resources, motivations, to-do checklists, and blogs.

After experiencing COVID and Virtual Learning, many students suffer from procrastination and motivation. This website can keep the users' motivated and manage their time effectively.

Resources

The Find Resources page of the website provides users internships and opportunities. In addition, there will be resources like Scholarships, Technology, Free Courses, Certificates to aid in students from underrepresented backgrounds.

Quotes

The Motivation Center page of the website provides users with quotes from Zen Quotes API, to keep the user motivated with coding, or even in daily life.

Checklist

The Planner page of the website provides users with a checklist feature that keeps track of things they want to do.
